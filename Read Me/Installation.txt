		
			***Installation***

	1.Import the "networking.sql" from the "DataBase Tables" folder to your phpMyAdmin.
	2.Turn on your MySQL service from XAMPP.
	3.Open the Client and Server projects by using "NetBeans" or "Eclips".
	4.Run the "Server.java" in "Server->Source Pakages->server". 
	5.Run the "Client.java" in "Client->Source Pakages->client". 
	6.Read the Read me file for more instructions.
